UX Designer

Houston based Graphic and Web designer working at expanding my skills in the field of User Experience Design.

Foodle

Foodle is a conceptual iOS application that takes research-driven data and crosses it with online content creation and collaboration. It allows users to find, create, photograph and share their favorite food and recipes with the world.

BusyBus

BusyBus is a conceptual app idea that explored how a city bus transit system would handle congestion on overlapping service lines. The solution was to be explored completely using the design process to document the proposed solution.
